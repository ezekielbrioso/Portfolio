Edgewharf Town

Bitsy Game

Edgewharf Town is a minimalist, top-down mystery adventure featuring a striking monochromatic pixel-art style. Playing as a detective, you investigate the legend of the "Moonlight Bandit," a figure blamed for local disappearances and nocturnal screams. The gameplay focuses on environmental exploration and clue-gathering, leading to the discovery of Lumina—a girl trapped in a supernatural loop. By solving puzzles and activating hidden symbols, you shift the narrative from an urban legend mystery into a mission to break a curse and restore the town's peace.
